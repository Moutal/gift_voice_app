## App Name

## Short Description

Record, organize, and export event gifts with voice-assisted AI.

## Full Description

Event Gift Keeper helps you track gifts at weddings, family events, parties, and business gatherings.

Speak naturally in Hebrew, Arabic, or English, and the app can turn your recording into organized gift entries. It supports money gifts in multiple currencies, regular gifts, giver names, totals, and exports.

Key features:

- Voice-assisted gift entry
- Hebrew, Arabic, and English speech support
- Multiple people in one recording
- Monetary totals grouped by currency
- Regular gift tracking
- PDF and CSV export
- Cloud sync for signed-in users
- Freemium plan with optional Premium upgrade

Use Event Gift Keeper to avoid messy notes, missing names, and manual gift calculations during important events.
